# Trading Strategies Knowledge Base

## RSI Divergence Reversal
Look for bearish divergence (price higher high + RSI lower high) near resistance, or bullish divergence near support. Enter on confirmation candle with stop beyond the swing point. RSI above 70 is overbought, below 30 is oversold.

## Golden Cross / Death Cross
When the 50 EMA crosses above the 200 EMA (golden cross), it signals a bullish trend shift. The reverse (death cross) signals bearish. Works best on daily and weekly timeframes. Institutional traders watch the 200 EMA religiously.

## EMA Pullback
In a trending market, wait for price to pull back to the 20 or 50 EMA, then enter in the trend direction when price bounces with a strong candle. The trend is your friend.

## MACD Histogram Reversal
When the histogram starts shrinking after a strong move, momentum is fading. Enter when the histogram flips color with volume confirmation. Zero-line crossovers are significant.

## Bollinger Band Squeeze Breakout
When bands contract to their narrowest point in 6+ months, a major move is loading. Enter on the breakout candle that closes outside the band with volume spike. The tighter the squeeze, the bigger the move.

## Bollinger Mean Reversion
In ranging markets, buy touches of the lower band and sell touches of the upper band, using the middle band (20 SMA) as your target.

## Fibonacci Confluence
Draw Fibonacci from swing low to swing high. When the 61.8% retracement aligns with a support zone, order block, or EMA, it creates a high-probability entry. The 61.8% (golden ratio) is the most powerful level.

## VWAP Reclaim
If price drops below VWAP and then reclaims it with strong volume, it signals institutional buying. Enter long on the reclaim with stop below VWAP. Key intraday level.

## Ichimoku Kumo Breakout
When price breaks above the cloud (Kumo) and the Tenkan-sen crosses above the Kijun-sen, it's a strong buy signal. All five lines aligning in one direction is the highest confidence setup. Cloud color indicates future trend.

## Stochastic + RSI Double Confirmation
When both Stochastic and RSI simultaneously reach oversold (below 20/30) at a key support level, enter long. This double-filter eliminates many false signals.

## ATR Chandelier Exit
Set your stop at 2x or 3x ATR below the highest close. This keeps you in trends during normal pullbacks while protecting profits. Trend followers use this to ride big moves.

## Key Principle: Confluence
No single indicator works in isolation. The highest-probability setups come from confluence: when 2-3 indicators from different categories (trend + momentum + volume) all agree at the same price level.
