# Market Regime Classification

## Bull Trend
- Price above 200 EMA, 50 EMA above 200 EMA
- Higher highs and higher lows on daily
- Funding rates positive (longs paying shorts)
- Volume increasing on up moves
- Strategy: Buy dips to EMAs, trend following, wider stops

## Bear Trend
- Price below 200 EMA, 50 EMA below 200 EMA
- Lower highs and lower lows on daily
- Funding rates negative (shorts paying longs)
- Volume increasing on down moves
- Strategy: Sell rallies, short breakdowns, tight stops

## Ranging / Consolidation
- Price oscillating between clear support and resistance
- Hurst exponent < 0.5 (mean reverting)
- Bollinger Bands contracting (squeeze forming)
- Low volume, decreasing ATR
- Strategy: Mean reversion at boundaries, Bollinger bounce

## Capitulation / Crash
- Extreme negative funding rates
- Massive liquidation cascades
- Volume spike 5x+ average
- VIX/fear gauges at extremes
- Strategy: Wait for reversal confirmation, don't catch falling knives

## Breakout / Expansion
- Bollinger Band squeeze resolves with volume
- Price breaks key resistance/support with conviction
- ATR expanding rapidly
- Open interest surging
- Strategy: Enter on breakout retest, use Chandelier exit for trailing stop

## Choppy / No-Trade
- No clear trend, frequent false breakouts
- Whipsawing around EMAs
- Low conviction from all indicators
- Strategy: Sit out, preserve capital, wait for clarity
